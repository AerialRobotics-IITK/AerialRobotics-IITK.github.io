About Us

Taking Off

We are a team of undergraduate students at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, working towards the development of autonomous aerial vehicles to implement various industrial challenges and contribute to the open-source robotics community.

Equipped with state-of-the-art techniques in the domain of aerial vehicles, we work in various fields including those of controls, localization and mapping to push the envelope of unmanned aerial vehicle technology.
